 **Sample Syllabus:** 

| HONORS FRENCH III This course is preparation for Advanced Placement French Language, and stresses proficiency in the four language skills. Materials are presented in unabridged and unedited forms: news reports and videos, essays, and literature. Listening and speaking skills are central to the course in the form of informational and personal presentations. The course is conducted almost entirely in the target language; regular participation is required. Giving advice, making hypotheses, and narrating with confidence between past, present, and future are the three core techniques students work on. Students who successfully complete this course should demonstrate Intermediate-High proficiency (level 6\) in at least 3 of the 4 ACTFL standards.   |
